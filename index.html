Bruce Banner is a renowmed scientist whit seven Ph.D.s and expertise in the field of gamma radiation. At Culver University in Virginia. The experiment-part of a world War II era "super soldier" program that Ross hopes to recreate-falls, and the exposure to gamma radiation causes Banner to transform into the Hulk for brief periods of time, whenever his heart rate rises above 200.
